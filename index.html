Ambient Noise Generator

Introduction

We live in a noisy world, and often times it is difficult to escape all that noise in our busy lives. Many people struggle with getting in the right headspace for productivity. There are so many distractions that is it difficult to focus when studying or doing projects, and oftentimes music and podcasts can take our attention away even when intended for focusing. Our goal is to give users the tools to create detailed and varied soundscapes for a number of applications. These applications include media projects involving sound (e.g. foley for a film), assistance for studying or sleeping, or helping an individual or group of people to relax (e.g. yoga or meditation). There are some websites that attempt to accomplish this, but we believe they don't go far enough or are limited in their scope. Our intention is to create a functional but pleasing interface that allows users to control the world of sound. We also have a number of features that intend to expand the functionality of this product such as presets, downloadable soundscapes, and being able to upload custom sounds. There's a lot of potential, and we want to ensure that our product is well-designed and accomplishes everything that a user could want from an ambient sound generator.

Key Stakeholders

This product provides users with a selection of high quality background sounds as well as a tool to assist with user customization. The primary users of this product would include those who want to introduce ambient white noise to their daily lives to assist with working or relaxing. These users would include students trying to study, people who have difficulty falling asleep, and people who are simply looking for an escape from a noisy environment. This product could also be valuable to those who desire a natural atmosphere but live in an urban environment. Other primary users of this application might be people working in the Foley departments of digital media. These users could use the app as a resource for finding background sound effects as well as a tool for audio editing.

Requirements

Requirement 1

The user should be able to independently control the volume of any of the ambient sounds.

Scenario

Todd has found a sound combination that he enjoys for relaxing but notices that the bird noises are louder then he would expect. Todd is able to dampen the sounds of the birds and balance them with the rest of his sounds.

Requirement 2

The user should be able to export an audio file, likely an mp3, based on their current sound selection, intensity, and volumes.

Scenario

Sarah has completed her selection of sounds for a scene and now wishes to export them in a high quality file to include as background noise. Sarah can click the download button and receive an audio file containing her exact selection of sounds. She can now easily add this sound file to her production.

Requirement 2.1

The user should be able to choose the length/format of the audio file that they are exporting.

Scenario

For the scene Sarah is creating she requires 3:14 of audio. During the export process Sarah selects this audio file length and completes the Foley for her scene.

Requirement 3

Ability for a user to change the user to change the intensity of a sound sample. For example, an ocean sound may have the option to increase how hard the waves are crashing onto the shore or to add or remove sea birds.

Scenario

Todd grew up near an ocean in Victoria but now is attending school in central Alberta. A recent writing assignment has him both stressed and at a loss of focus. Todd, to relax himself and remind himself of home, puts on the sound of gentle waves hitting the beach.

Requirement 4

There should be an option that allows the users to upload any sound sample that they want. The uploaded sample will have the same volume control option as all the default sound samples but will not have an intensity option. The file format will be restricted to specific audio codecs in order to filter out bad files.
