Ambient Noise Generator

Requirements

Requirement 1

The user should be able to independently control the volume of any of the ambient sounds.

Scenario

Todd has found a sound combination that he enjoys for relaxing but notices that the bird noises are louder then he would expect. Todd is able to dampen the sounds of the birds and balance them with the rest of his sounds.

Requirement 2

The user should be able to export an audio file, likely an mp3, based on their current sound selection, intensity, and volumes.

Scenario

Sarah has completed her selection of sounds for a scene and now wishes to export them in a high quality file to include as background noise. Sarah can click the download button and receive an audio file containing her exact selection of sounds. She can now easily add this sound file to her production.

Requirement 2.1

The user should be able to choose the length/format of the audio file that they are exporting.

Scenario

For the scene Sarah is creating she requires 3:14 of audio. During the export process Sarah selects this audio file length and completes the Foley for her scene.

Requirement 3

Ability for a user to change the user to change the intensity of a sound sample. For example, an ocean sound may have the option to increase how hard the waves are crashing onto the shore or to add or remove sea birds.

Scenario

Todd grew up near an ocean in Victoria but now is attending school in central Alberta. A recent writing assignment has him both stressed and at a loss of focus. Todd, to relax himself and remind himself of home, puts on the sound of gentle waves hitting the beach.

Requirement 4

There should be an option that allows the users to upload any sound sample that they want. The uploaded sample will have the same volume control option as all the default sound samples but will not have an intensity option. The file format will be restricted to specific audio codecs in order to filter out bad files.
